About

A brief breakdown of what got me here.

When COVID-19 hit in early 2020, I lost my job as a Service Technician in the construction industry. This gave me the opportunity to reflect on my working life thus far, and I decided it was time to move in a direction that centred around my interests.“What do I really want to do with myself?" I thought, "What makes me happy?”. I am a naturally creative person and have always loved computers, so a few weeks, and many google searches later I joined the Software Engineering Course at General Assembly and completely immersed myself in Coding. I truely believe it was one of the greatest decisions of my life.



Interests

  • Brazilian Jiu Jitsu / martial arts: I have been training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu for 11 years where I've reached the rank of brown belt. I've coached both adults and children, and have cornered MMA fighters to international titles.
  • Creating: I've always been a creative person, whether it was LEGO and art as a kid or Coding now as an adult. I love the process of imagining an idea and bringing it to reality.
  • Exercise: Physical fitness has always been part of my life. As a kid you would find me on the monkey bars or racing someone to see who was the fastest. I have both my Cert 3 and Cert 4 in fitness and was a performance coach for the UFC gym where I taught strength and conditioning and martial arts.
